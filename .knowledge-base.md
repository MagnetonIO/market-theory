### Glossary of Core Concepts

- **Belief Function**: Probability distribution over future states, formed by aggregating heterogeneous private signals via Bayesian updating
- **Market Temperature**: T = aggregate uncertainty; governs Boltzmann distribution form
- **Free Energy**: F = ⟨E⟩ - TS (thermodynamic) or F[μ] = E_μ[-log p(D|ω)] + KL(μ||π_0) (predictive)
- **Predictive Measure (P_pred)**: Market's collective forecast, purged of risk distortions
- **Risk Distortion Kernel (R)**: Maps predictive measure to risk-neutral pricing measure
- **Phase Transition**: Discontinuous change in belief distribution → crash
- **Precision**: τ_i = 1/σ²_i; inverse variance of agent i's signal
- **Entropic Liquidity**: Λ = exp(H[belief distribution]); market depth from belief diversity
- **Variational Inference**: Market finds P_pred by minimizing predictive free energy
- **UPMF**: Complete specification: state space, agents, energy, temperature, partition function, predictive measure, risk kernel, pricing functional, dynamics, thermodynamic constraints
